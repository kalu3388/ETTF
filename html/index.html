About Us

The Ethiopian Table Tennis Federation (ETTF) is the official governing body for table tennis in Ethiopia. Our mission is to develop, promote, and regulate the sport across the nation, nurturing talents and fostering sportsmanship.

Competition is organised across two divisions (Premier & Division 1) to ensure balanced growth for clubs and athletes country‑wide.

Tournament Formats

National titles are contested in the following divisions & formats:

Men’s Doubles

Two‑player men’s pairs; best of five sets.

Women’s Doubles

Elite women pairings face off for national honours.

Mixed Doubles

One man + one woman teaming up—perfect balance of power and precision.

Men’s Team

Club squads of four compete in singles & doubles rubbers.

Women’s Team

Women’s club line‑ups battle for supremacy.
